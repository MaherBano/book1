# Chapter 1: Introduction to AI Concepts

Welcome to the first chapter of our journey into Artificial Intelligence. This chapter introduces fundamental AI concepts and terminology that will serve as building blocks for more advanced topics in later chapters.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you will:
- Understand what Artificial Intelligence is
- Recognize common AI techniques and applications
- Identify the differences between various AI approaches

## Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines programmed to think and learn like humans. This technology powers everything from voice recognition systems to recommendation algorithms used by streaming services.
