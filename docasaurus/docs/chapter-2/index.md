# Chapter 2: Fundamentals of Robotics

This chapter introduces you to the core concepts of robotics and automation. Building on the AI concepts from Chapter 1, we'll explore how intelligent systems are embodied in physical robots.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you will:
- Understand the basic components of robotic systems
- Recognize different types of robots and their applications
- Grasp the principles of robot control and navigation

## Overview

Robotics combines mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, and computer science to design, construct, operate, and apply robots. These machines are increasingly becoming part of our daily lives, from industrial applications to home assistants.
